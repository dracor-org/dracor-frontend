# Get in Touch

You may contact the DraCor team for general questions via **info@dracor.org**. This email address is curated by Luca Giovannini, Peer Trilcke, and Frank Fischer. For questions related to a specific corpus, please contact the respective editors (see the [corpus registry](https://dracor.org/doc/corpora)).  

We also maintain a [**DraCor mailing list**](https://www.listserv.dfn.de/sympa/info/dracormailinglist) that you may join to share any DraCor-related content, such as upcoming talks, recent publications, or questions you would like to discuss with the DraCor community.  

For informal exchange, we have a [**Mattermost**](https://mattermost.com/) team hosted by the University of Potsdam. If you would like to join the DraCor community there, please write an email to info@dracor.org to receive further information.  

Another way to engage with DraCor is via **GitHub** discussions or GitHub issues. For instance, you may discuss the [DraCor Frontend](https://github.com/dracor-org/dracor-frontend/discussions) or the [DraCor API](https://github.com/dracor-org/dracor-api/discussions).  

Regarding social media, DraCor also has a [**Bluesky**](https://bsky.app/profile/dracor.org) account. You may follow the account to receive news about DraCor or link to it in your own DraCor-related posts.  
Preferred hashtags are #DraCor #ProgrammableCorpora #DigitalHumanities.
